Vizualizing Navient Corproation Complaint Data

Created By Isaiah Hayes

Based on data from the Consumer Compliant Database and The Navient Lawsuits: Large Settlement for Some, No Relief for Others by Clint Proctor.

The Navient Corporation has dealt with numerous lawsuits since 2017. These suits came from many angles, including the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB), state attorneys, and teachers.

The complaints that were filed are listed in the Consumer Complaint Database. The trends of these complaints depict the largest issues and products that did not leave consumers happy. According to the data, there were almost 400,000 complaints filed jsut within the time span of 5/15/2022 to 11/15/2022 in the United States.
